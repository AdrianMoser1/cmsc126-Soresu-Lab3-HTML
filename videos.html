The video features Tribu Kabugwason which is a dance performed during Miag-ao's Salakayan Festival. Their name, derived from a Kinaray-a term associated with waterways, symbolizes life and movement. The performance focuses on the "warrior spirit" of the Miag-ao people, featuring rhythmic beats and synchronized choreography that tells the story of local resilience.
